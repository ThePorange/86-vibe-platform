# 1. Purpose

This document identifies the strategic risks associated with the design, implementation and long-term operation of the 86-vibe platform.

The objective is to recognise significant risks early and establish appropriate mitigation strategies.

The Risk Register SHALL be reviewed and updated throughout the lifecycle of the platform.

---

# 2. Risk Management Objectives

Risk management SHALL:

- improve decision making
- reduce uncertainty
- increase platform resilience
- protect architectural integrity
- support long-term sustainability
- improve engineering quality

Risk management is intended to support informed decision making rather than eliminate all uncertainty.

---

# 3. Risk Management Principles

The following principles govern risk management.

## RM-001 Early Identification

Risks SHOULD be identified as early as practical.

---

## RM-002 Transparency

Known risks SHALL be documented.

---

## RM-003 Ownership

Every significant risk SHALL have an identified owner.

---

## RM-004 Mitigation

Where practical, mitigation strategies SHALL be defined before implementation.

---

## RM-005 Continuous Review

Risks SHALL be reviewed periodically and updated as circumstances change.

---

# 4. Risk Assessment Method

Risks are assessed using two dimensions.

### Probability

| Rating | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Low | Unlikely |
| Medium | Possible |
| High | Likely |

---

### Impact

| Rating | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Low | Limited effect |
| Medium | Noticeable impact |
| High | Significant impact on platform objectives |

Overall priority is determined through professional judgement using both probability and impact.

# 6. Risk Review Process

Strategic risks SHALL be reviewed at the following points.

- Architecture review
- Major release planning
- Annual roadmap review
- Significant technology change
- Introduction of new external integrations

Additional reviews MAY be conducted where significant new risks are identified.

Changes to strategic risks SHOULD be documented through normal governance processes.

---

# 7. Risk Ownership

Risk ownership is assigned according to accountability.

| Owner | Responsibility |
|-------|----------------|
| Product Owner | Product, governance, commercial and release risks |
| Chief Systems Architect | Architecture, technology and engineering risks |
| Future Engineering Teams | Operational risks within implementation projects |

Ownership of a risk includes responsibility for monitoring, mitigation planning and periodic review.

Delegation of mitigation activities SHALL NOT remove accountability.

---

# 8. Summary

The Risk Register provides a structured view of the principal strategic risks facing the 86-vibe platform.

By identifying, documenting and reviewing these risks throughout the lifecycle of the platform, 86-vibe aims to preserve architectural integrity, engineering quality and long-term sustainability.

Risk management SHALL remain an ongoing activity throughout the evolution of the platform.
