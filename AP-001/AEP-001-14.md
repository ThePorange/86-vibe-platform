# 1. Purpose

This document defines the strategic metrics used to measure the effectiveness of the 86-vibe platform.

The objective is to establish measurable indicators that support continuous improvement while ensuring alignment with the Product Vision and Design Principles.

These metrics measure the platform itself rather than the business success of projects created using the platform.

---

# 2. Measurement Objectives

The success metrics SHALL:

- support objective decision making
- measure platform effectiveness
- identify opportunities for improvement
- demonstrate engineering quality
- measure AI contribution
- monitor governance compliance
- provide long-term trend analysis

Metrics SHALL support improvement rather than individual performance assessment.

---

# 3. Measurement Principles

The following principles apply to all platform metrics.

## SM-001 Relevance

Every metric SHALL support a defined business or engineering objective.

---

## SM-002 Measurability

Metrics SHALL be objectively measurable.

---

## SM-003 Actionability

Metrics SHOULD enable informed improvement decisions.

---

## SM-004 Simplicity

Metrics SHOULD be easy to understand.

---

## SM-005 Continuous Improvement

Metrics SHALL support trend analysis over time rather than isolated measurement.

---

# 4. Success Categories

Success metrics are organised into five categories.

- Platform
- Engineering
- AI
- Product
- Governance

Each category represents a different aspect of platform effectiveness.

# 10. Review Process

Platform metrics SHOULD be reviewed regularly.

Recommended review cadence:

| Activity | Frequency |
|----------|-----------|
| Engineering metrics | Per sprint or iteration |
| AI metrics | Monthly |
| Product metrics | Monthly |
| Governance metrics | Quarterly |
| Strategic platform review | Annually |

Metric definitions MAY evolve through approved governance processes while maintaining historical traceability.

Where practical, metric collection SHOULD be automated.

---

# 11. Summary

The Success Metrics defined within this document provide a structured framework for evaluating the effectiveness of the 86-vibe platform.

By measuring platform capability, engineering quality, AI contribution, product delivery and governance compliance, 86-vibe supports evidence-based continuous improvement.

These metrics SHALL guide future platform evolution while remaining aligned with the Product Vision, Design Principles and Governance Model established within Architecture Package AP-001.
