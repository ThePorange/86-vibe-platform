# 1. Purpose

This document defines the long-term vision for 86-vibe and establishes the principles that guide every architectural and product decision.

While implementation details, technologies and AI models will evolve over time, the vision described within this document is intended to remain stable across multiple major releases.

Every feature proposed for 86-vibe should support this vision.

---

# 2. Vision Statement

**86-vibe will become the operating system for AI-assisted software engineering.**

The platform will provide a structured, governed and repeatable framework that enables humans and AI agents to collaborate throughout the complete software development lifecycle.

Rather than acting as another coding assistant, 86-vibe will orchestrate engineering activities across planning, architecture, implementation, testing, documentation, deployment and continuous improvement.

The platform should enable organisations of all sizes to build software with greater consistency, higher quality and increased engineering confidence.

---

# 3. Mission Statement

The mission of 86-vibe is to transform AI from an isolated coding assistant into a coordinated engineering team operating within a professional software delivery framework.

86-vibe seeks to:

- improve engineering quality
- reduce repetitive work
- preserve engineering knowledge
- increase development velocity
- maintain architectural integrity
- provide complete traceability
- support continuous improvement

while ensuring that humans retain ownership of strategic decisions.

---

# 4. Product Philosophy

86-vibe is founded upon the belief that successful software projects depend upon engineering discipline rather than code generation.

AI should accelerate engineering rather than replace engineering.

The platform therefore treats software development as a sequence of governed engineering activities rather than a sequence of AI prompts.

Every activity should produce reusable knowledge.

Every engineering decision should be explainable.

Every significant change should be traceable.

Every release should be reproducible.

---

# 5. Core Values

## Human Leadership

Humans remain responsible for vision, product direction, priorities and approval.

AI assists humans rather than replacing them.

---

## Engineering Excellence

Quality takes precedence over speed.

Automation should improve quality rather than bypass engineering discipline.

---

## Transparency

Every decision should be understandable.

Users should always know:

- why something exists
- who approved it
- how it was implemented
- what changed
- when it changed

---

## Continuous Learning

Projects should become more effective over time.

Lessons learned from previous projects should improve future projects.

---

## Simplicity

Solutions should be as simple as practical while remaining extensible.

Complexity should only be introduced when justified by measurable value.

---

## Vendor Neutrality

86-vibe should minimise dependencies upon specific vendors wherever practical.

The platform should be capable of adopting new AI providers, development tools and technologies with minimal architectural impact.

---

# 6. Strategic Objectives

The long-term objectives of 86-vibe are:

## Standardise AI Engineering

Provide a repeatable engineering methodology that can be applied across every software project.

---

## Improve Software Quality

Increase software quality through:

- architecture-first development
- automated testing
- documentation
- review workflows
- engineering governance

---

## Reduce Time to Value

Reduce the effort required to transform an idea into a production-ready application.

---

## Preserve Organisational Knowledge

Capture engineering knowledge throughout the software lifecycle.

Knowledge should survive changes in personnel, AI models and development tools.

---

## Support Multiple AI Providers

Enable intelligent routing between AI models based upon task complexity, cost and capability.

---

## Build Reusable Engineering Assets

Every completed project should contribute reusable templates, workflows and best practices back into the platform.

### Principle 1

Architecture precedes implementation.

---

### Principle 2

---

### Principle 3

---

### Principle 4

---

### Principle 5

Humans approve significant decisions.

---

Every implementation must be traceable.

---

---

# 8. Guiding Statements

When uncertainty exists, the following statements should guide decision making.

> Build systems that can evolve.

> Prefer standards over convention.

> Prefer automation over manual repetition.

> Prefer documentation over tribal knowledge.

> Prefer reusable components over one-off solutions.

> Prefer explicit decisions over hidden assumptions.

> Prefer measurable quality over perceived productivity.

> Keep humans responsible for product decisions.

> Make engineering knowledge permanent.

> Build platforms, not collections of prompts.

---

# 9. Long-Term Vision

The long-term ambition for 86-vibe extends beyond individual software projects.

Future versions should support:

- multiple development teams
- enterprise governance
- portfolio management
- engineering analytics
- reusable AI agents
- organisational knowledge management
- engineering standards management
- cross-project learning
- autonomous engineering workflows operating within defined governance boundaries

Ultimately, 86-vibe should become an extensible engineering platform capable of supporting software development across organisations of varying size and complexity.

---

# 10. Success Definition

The vision of 86-vibe will be considered successful when:

- software projects consistently begin with structured architecture
- AI-generated software remains maintainable years after creation
- engineering documentation remains synchronised with implementation
- multiple AI providers can participate within a common engineering workflow
- new projects can be created rapidly using proven engineering assets
- engineering quality improves over successive projects
- organisations trust AI as a governed engineering participant rather than an uncontrolled code generator

---

# Vision Summary

86-vibe is not intended to become another AI coding assistant.

Its purpose is to become the engineering platform that coordinates humans, AI agents, engineering knowledge and software delivery into a single governed operating model.

Every feature developed for 86-vibe should strengthen that operating model.

When architectural choices become difficult, the preferred option should always be the one that most closely supports this vision.
